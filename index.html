Our Story

Introduction Sri Sathyavani Vidya Peetha was established in 2001 with the blessings of His Holiness Dr. D. V. Veerendra Heggade of Shree Kshethra Dharmasthala. Rooted in the teachings of Sri Ramakrishna Paramahamsa and Swami Vivekananda, the institution is committed to uplifting socially and economically underprivileged communities while providing quality education.

FACULTY



Principal

Dr. N V DEVI PRASAD SHETTY

(M.A,M.Phil,M.ED.,P.G.D.A.,Ph.D.)

Our Principal, Dr. N.V. Devi Prasad Shetty, an accomplished educationist and administrator, serves as the Director of Sri Sathyavani Vidya Peetha Group of Institutions. A gold medalist in M.A. and B.Ed., he holds multiple degrees, including M.Phil., M.Ed., P.G.D.A., and Ph.D. in student psychology. With extensive experience as a teacher, principal, and director, he has significantly contributed to education and administration. He has authored several research articles, books, and dramas, focusing on folklore, literature, and psychology.

A skilled orator and trainer, Dr. Shetty has conducted numerous lectures and workshops on personality development, teaching methodologies, and child psychology. He has also been an active contributor to cultural arts, with expertise in Yakshagana, drama, and literary analysis. A recipient of numerous prestigious awards, including the Shikshana Bheeshma and Best Administrator 2024, he continues to inspire with his dedication to academics, leadership, and cultural preservation.
